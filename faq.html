Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about shopping with KGS Home Décors.

local_shippingOrders & Delivery

How long does delivery take?

We deliver within Virudhachalam free of charge — same day or next day for most in-stock items. For orders outside Virudhachalam, delivery typically takes 3–7 business days depending on your location.

Do you offer free delivery?

Yes! We offer free delivery for every order within Virudhachalam — no minimum order value required. For orders outside Virudhachalam, shipping charges may apply based on the destination and order size.

Can I track my order?

Absolutely. Once your order is dispatched, you can track it on our Track Order page. You'll also receive updates via WhatsApp or SMS.

What if I'm not home at the time of delivery?

Our delivery team will call before arriving. If you miss the delivery, we'll attempt it again the next day. You can also reschedule by contacting us on WhatsApp.

Can I change or cancel my order after placing it?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your order within 2 hours of placing it by contacting us on WhatsApp at +91 97891 82921. After that, your order may already be packed and ready to dispatch.

replayReturns & Refunds

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for items that are damaged, defective, or significantly different from what was described. Please read our full Returns Policy for details.

How do I initiate a return?

To start a return, contact us on WhatsApp or email us within 7 days of receiving your order. Share photos of the item and describe the issue. We'll arrange a pickup at no extra cost for valid returns.

When will I receive my refund?

Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item. The amount will be credited back to the original payment method used at checkout.

What items cannot be returned?

The following items are non-returnable:

  • Items that have been used, assembled, or installed
  • Custom or made-to-order products
  • Items damaged due to mishandling after delivery
  • Products without original packaging

paymentsPayments

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major payment methods through our secure Razorpay gateway:

  • Credit & Debit Cards — Visa, Mastercard, Amex, RuPay
  • UPI — PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm, BHIM, and all UPI apps
  • Net Banking — all major Indian banks
  • Wallets — Paytm, Mobikwik, and others

See our Payment Options page for full details.

Is it safe to pay on your website?

Yes, completely. All payments are processed through Razorpay, a PCI DSS Level 1 compliant payment gateway. Your card details are never stored on our servers — they go directly to Razorpay's secure vault. All transactions are encrypted with industry-standard SSL.

My payment failed but money was deducted. What should I do?

This can happen when a payment is interrupted mid-transaction. The amount is usually auto-refunded within 3–5 business days by your bank. If it's not refunded after 5 days, please contact us on WhatsApp with your transaction reference and we'll resolve it promptly.

categoryProducts

Are your products the same as shown in photos?

We photograph every product in our actual showroom under natural light. Colours may appear slightly different on different screens — but we never use edited or misleading images. What you see is what you get.

Can I see the product in person before buying?

Yes! Visit our showroom at 185/G Junction Road, Virudhachalam 606001. We're open 10 AM – 10 PM, every day. Our staff will be happy to help you find the right piece for your home.

How do I care for my décor items?

Care instructions vary by product type. General guidelines:

  • Resin & ceramic: Wipe with a dry or lightly damp cloth. Avoid harsh cleaners.
  • Artificial plants: Dust regularly with a soft brush or cloth.
  • Metal items: Wipe dry after cleaning to prevent tarnishing.
  • Wall frames & clocks: Keep away from direct moisture and sunlight.
Do you do custom or bulk orders?

Yes! We handle bulk orders for events, gifts, and interior projects. For custom or large orders, please contact us directly on WhatsApp or visit our store. We offer special pricing for bulk purchases.

manage_accountsAccount & Orders

Do I need an account to place an order?

Yes, creating a free account allows you to track your orders, save your wishlist, and manage your addresses. Create an account — it only takes a minute.

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

Go to the Forgot Password page, enter your registered email, and we'll send you a reset link. Check your spam folder if you don't see it within a few minutes.

How do I view my past orders?

Sign in to your account and go to My Account → My Orders. You'll see a full history of your purchases along with their current status.

Still have questions?

We're here to help — reach us on WhatsApp, call us, or visit the store.

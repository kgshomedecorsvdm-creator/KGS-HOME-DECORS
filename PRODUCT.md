# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Homeowners, interior design enthusiasts, and premium shoppers in Virudhachalam and Tamil Nadu looking for export-quality, luxury home décor, furniture, and unique lifestyle accessories. They value aesthetics, exclusivity, and tactile quality.

## Product Purpose
To serve as the digital flagship storefront for KGS Home Décors. It must seamlessly convert online visitors into buyers by exuding the same premium, curated experience they would feel walking into the physical showroom.

## Brand Personality
Luxurious, Curated, Confident.

## Anti-references
- Standard Shopify themes with endless identical square grids.
- Loud, aggressive e-commerce "SALE" banners in primary red.
- Over-cluttered navigation and aggressive WhatsApp floating popups.

## Design Principles
- **Show, Don't Tell**: Let the high-quality imagery of the furniture and decor take up maximum space.
- **Breathe**: Use extreme macro-whitespace to separate sections and create pacing.
- **Physicality**: UI elements should feel like machined hardware or physical objects (using the double-bezel technique and soft shadows).

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast for readability (passing WCAG AA for text).
- Avoid strobe/flashing animations; use smooth cubic-bezier easing.

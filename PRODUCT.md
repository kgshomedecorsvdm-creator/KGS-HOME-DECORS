## Product Name
KGS Home Décors

## Tagline
Luxury Home Décor Crafted For Modern Spaces

## Product Purpose
Premium furniture and home décor e-commerce store serving customers across India, with a flagship showroom at 185/G Junction Road, Virudhachalam 606001, Tamil Nadu. Ships across India; free delivery within Virudhachalam. Sells hand-curated pieces — sofas, beds, dining sets, wall art, clocks, lighting, rugs, and décor accents.

## Register
brand — design IS the product. The store's visual identity communicates premium quality and trust before a single product is browsed.

## Users

### Primary: Home Owner (25–50, Tier 2/3 India)
- Furnishing or upgrading their home
- Wants quality products they can trust, not cheap mass-market imports
- Discovers via WhatsApp shares, Instagram, word-of-mouth
- Checks price carefully; COD and UPI are preferred payment methods
- Needs reassurance: real photos, clear returns, local delivery proof

### Secondary: Gift Buyer (30–55)
- Shopping for housewarming gifts, wedding presents
- Wants curated, premium-looking items in a gift-appropriate price band
- Responds to "gifting" framing, badges like "Popular" and "Trending"

### Tertiary: Interior Designer / Small Business
- Buys in quantity for client projects
- Wants to browse the full catalog and filter by category quickly

## Brand Voice
- Warm but authoritative
- Never pushy or salesy
- Speaks like a knowledgeable friend who curates beautiful things
- Tamil Nadu roots but pan-India aspirations
- Avoids: corporate buzzwords, excessive exclamation marks, cheap discount language

---

## Design Principles

1. **Premium before price** — Every pixel communicates craft and quality. No cluttered sales banners.
2. **Warmth is the brand** — Bone paper, antique gold, terracotta. Never cold, clinical, or sterile.
3. **Typography carries weight** — Crimson Pro (serif display) + Jost (sans body). Never swap these.
4. **Touch-first** — 60%+ of visits are mobile. Every interaction must feel effortless on a phone.
5. **Trust is earned visually** — Hand-checked badges, delivery clarity, WhatsApp access reduce friction.

## Anti-References (what KGS must NOT look like)
- Generic Shopify themes with purple gradients and neon CTAs
- Amazon-style information overload with badges everywhere
- Cheap discount-store aesthetic (flashing prices, red stickers)
- Cold minimalism with no warmth or texture (IKEA clinical white)
- AI-generated stock photos with suspiciously perfect scenes

---

## Strategic Principles
- COD and UPI must always be visible — they are conversion-critical for this audience
- WhatsApp access (wa.me/919789182921) is the #1 customer support channel
- Free delivery badge "In Virudhachalam" builds local trust
- Product images must feel hand-chosen, not catalog-generated
- Reviews and ratings are trust anchors — show them prominently

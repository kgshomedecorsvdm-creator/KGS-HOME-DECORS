## Design Context

### Users
Everyone who cares about their home — local families in Virudhachalam and surrounding Tamil Nadu towns furnishing or upgrading their spaces, gift buyers shopping for weddings/housewarmings/festivals, and style-aware individuals who want their home to look a certain way. The store is a physical showroom on Junction Road; the website is its digital counterpart, trusted by 240+ Google reviewers.

### Brand Personality
**Calm · Editorial · Minimal**

KGS is a premium home décor brand that lets its products speak. The experience should feel like walking into a well-curated showroom — unhurried, confident, tasteful. Never shouty or cluttered. Think a beautifully lit Pepperfry or Urban Ladder spread: generous white space, strong lifestyle photography, serif-led editorial hierarchy, and warm neutral tones that feel expensive without being cold.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: Light. Bone/cream surfaces (`#FAF8F4`), warm ink text (`#1A1A1A`), antique gold accent (`#B89657`, `#C5A880`), terracotta CTA (`#C97840`).
- **Typography**: Crimson Pro (serif display, headlines) + Jost (sans-serif, UI/labels). Hierarchy through size contrast, not weight clutter.
- **Photography-first**: Lifestyle imagery carries the brand. UI exists to frame it, not compete with it.
- **Anti-references**: Not festive/colourful like Snapdeal or Meesho. Not cold/corporate. Not generic AI white-card grid with drop shadows everywhere.
- **Mobile**: Full-screen, image-dominant. Editorial magazine spread, not a shrunk desktop.

### Design Principles
1. **Let the product breathe** — whitespace is not wasted space. Every element earns its place.
2. **Editorial over commercial** — headlines, not banners. Photography, not product dumps.
3. **Warm restraint** — the palette is warm but the layout is controlled. Emotion through imagery, calm through spacing.
4. **Mobile is a first-class experience** — not a shrunk desktop. Redesign for the hand and the eye.
5. **Trust through honesty** — no fake reviews, fake stock counts, fake urgency. Premium feel comes from quality, not tricks.
